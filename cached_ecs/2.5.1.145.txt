ENTRY       EC 2.5.1.145                Enzyme
NAME        phosphatidylglycerol---prolipoprotein diacylglyceryl transferase;
            lgt (gene name)
CLASS       Transferases;
            Transferring alkyl or aryl groups, other than methyl groups;
            Transferring alkyl or aryl groups, other than methyl groups (only sub-subclass identified to date)
SYSNAME     L-1-phosphatidyl-sn-glycerol:[prolipoprotein]-L-cysteine diacyl-sn-glyceryltransferase
REACTION    L-1-phosphatidyl-sn-glycerol + a [prolipoprotein]-L-cysteine = sn-glycerol 1-phosphate + an [prolipoprotein]-S-1,2-diacyl-sn-glyceryl-L-cysteine
SUBSTRATE   L-1-phosphatidyl-sn-glycerol;
            [prolipoprotein]-L-cysteine
PRODUCT     sn-glycerol 1-phosphate [CPD:C00623];
            [prolipoprotein]-S-1,2-diacyl-sn-glyceryl-L-cysteine
COMMENT     This bacterial enzyme, which is associated with the membrane, catalyses the transfer of an sn-1,2-diacylglyceryl group from phosphatidylglycerol to the sulfhydryl group of the prospective N-terminal cysteine of a prolipoprotein, the first step in the formation of mature triacylated lipoproteins.
HISTORY     EC 2.5.1.145 created 2018
