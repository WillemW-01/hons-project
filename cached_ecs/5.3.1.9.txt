            Interconverting aldoses and ketoses, and related compounds
SYSNAME     alpha-D-glucose-6-phosphate aldose-ketose-isomerase (configuration-inverting)
REACTION    alpha-D-glucose 6-phosphate = beta-D-fructofuranose 6-phosphate [RN:R02740]
ALL_REAC    R02740;
            (other) R00771 R02739 R03321 R13199
SUBSTRATE   alpha-D-glucose 6-phosphate [CPD:C00668]
PRODUCT     beta-D-fructofuranose 6-phosphate [CPD:C05345]
COMMENT     The enzyme from yeast catalyses the reversible conversion specifically between the alpha-D-glucose 6-phosphate and beta-D-fructofuranose 6-phosphate. The enzyme also catalyses the anomerization of both D-hexose 6-phosphates [7].
