ENTRY       EC 2.3.1.234                Enzyme
NAME        N6-L-threonylcarbamoyladenine synthase;
            t6A synthase;
            Kae1;
            ygjD (gene name);
            Qri7
CLASS       Transferases;
            Acyltransferases;
            Transferring groups other than aminoacyl groups
SYSNAME     L-threonylcarbamoyladenylate:adenine37 in tRNA N6-L-threonylcarbamoyltransferase
REACTION    L-threonylcarbamoyladenylate + adenine37 in tRNA = AMP + N6-L-threonylcarbamoyladenine37 in tRNA [RN:R10648]
ALL_REAC    R10648
SUBSTRATE   L-threonylcarbamoyladenylate [CPD:C20641];
            adenine37 in tRNA [CPD:C17324]
PRODUCT     AMP [CPD:C00020];
            N6-L-threonylcarbamoyladenine37 in tRNA [CPD:C20751]
COMMENT     The enzyme is involved in the synthesis of N6-threonylcarbamoyladenosine37 in tRNAs, which is found in tRNAs with the anticodon NNU, i.e. tRNAIle, tRNAThr, tRNAAsn, tRNALys, tRNASer and tRNAArg [3].
