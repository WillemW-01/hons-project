ENTRY       EC 3.5.1.88                 Enzyme
NAME        peptide deformylase;
            N-formylmethionylaminoacyl-tRNA deformylase
CLASS       Hydrolases;
            Acting on carbon-nitrogen bonds, other than peptide bonds;
            In linear amides
SYSNAME     formyl-L-methionyl peptide amidohydrolase
REACTION    formyl-L-methionyl peptide + H2O = formate + methionyl peptide [RN:R05635]
ALL_REAC    R05635;
            (other) R04268
SUBSTRATE   formyl-L-methionyl peptide [CPD:C11439];
            H2O [CPD:C00001]
PRODUCT     formate [CPD:C00058];
            methionyl peptide [CPD:C11440]
COMMENT     Requires iron(II). Also requires at least a dipeptide for an efficient rate of reaction. N-terminal L-methionine is a prerequisite for activity but the enzyme has broad specificity at other positions. Differs in substrate specifity from EC 3.5.1.31 (formylmethionine deformylase).
HISTORY     EC 3.5.1.88 created 2001
